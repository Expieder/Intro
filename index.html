✨ What is Expieder?

Expieder is an Augmented Reality platform enabling spatial social interactions. Pin messages, media, and profiles to real-world locations — discover them as you move through your surroundings.

Whether outside your favorite café or along a mountain trail, leave behind a part of your story for others to uncover.
